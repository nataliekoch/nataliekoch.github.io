PawFinder

PawFinder is a web application I created at Prime Academy in two weeks.

I was looking into adopting a pet of my own, and I felt that the process could be greatly improved. I didn’t like that you had to click to navigate to see more pets, so I designed it load pets automatically as you scroll. Another problem I found was that there weren’t any indicators for pets with special needs or compatibility issues, so I while you scroll through pets, you’ll see bright indexes under pets that have those issues. Lastly, I found that sites require you to register an account to use the search feature, so on Paw Finder––it's optional.
