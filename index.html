PawFinder

•

Front-End, Back-End, and Design

After becoming frustrated with other pet adoption sites, I wanted to create something that would make the process easier.

Pawfinder allows users to browse for a particular breed of cat or dog in your local area within a masonry-styled interface. Each pet profile focuses on delivering the most important info for searching through pets quickly.

Equipment Manager

•

Front-End Design and Logic

The Equipment Manger app allows users with a Sports Engine account to upload their equipment inventory. Along with a team of fellow students, we created a mobile-first application where users can check out equipment for games and events pulled from their teams online calendar.

My primary focus for this project was designing and styling the application in accordance with Sports Engine’s style guide with a strong emphasis on mobile friendliness.
